Description


YouTube is a video-sharing platform where users can upload, view, and interact with videos posted by other users. It primarily serves as a platform for sharing and consuming video content rather than selling products or services.

It provides a platform for content creators to share their videos with a global audience, covering diverse topics such as entertainment, music, education, tutorials, vlogs, and more. Users can subscribe to channels, like and comment on videos, create playlists, and engage with the community. YouTube has become a significant source of entertainment, information, and creativity on the internet.
